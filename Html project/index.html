Capture Moments Instantly with Polaroid SX-70!

The Polaroid SX-70 is a legendary folding instant camera, originally released in 1972 by Edwin Land. With its unique design and instant photo development, the SX-70 revolutionized photography and became an iconic symbol of vintage instant imaging.

Our collection features genuine and carefully restored Polaroid SX-70 cameras, so you can experience the magic of instant photography in the digital age.
